# Hackathon Pitch: TourInHand

**The Pitch**
TourInHand v2 = AI-powered safe, smart, and sustainable travel planner for students and budget travelers

**Problem**
Students and budget travelers often struggle to find safe, eco-friendly, and affordable travel itineraries. Existing planners are either too expensive, lack sustainability scores, or fail to prioritize safety for young travelers.

**Solution**
TourInHand provides an intuitive, glassmorphism-styled dashboard that offers:
- Safety ratings and emergency contacts
- Eco-scores for sustainable choices
- Budget-friendly logistics and local hidden gems

**Target Audience**
Students and budget-conscious backpackers.
